👋 About the Role
Here's exactly what this job looks like
We believe in full transparency. Read this before applying.
A stable job, not random shifts

Most cleaning companies call you when they need you. At itsGLO, you have a set schedule, a reliable income, and a team behind you. We train you properly, give you checklists, and promote from within. If you show up consistently and take pride in your work — you will grow here.

  • 🏠 Professional condo & home cleaning — structured checklists for every job
  • 📋 3 types: Routine cleans, Deep cleans, Move-In / Move-Out cleans
  • 🕗 Typical hours: 8:30 AM – 5:30 PM. Some days end earlier or later.
  • 🎒 You carry a supply backpack or cart (~6 kg). We provide everything in it.
  • 🚌 Travel by TTC or your own vehicle. Far trips: we arrange a ride between jobs.
  • 💰 Paid every other Friday. Travel time between jobs is paid.
  • ❌ This is NOT maid service. No laundry, dishes, cooking, ironing, or pet care.
⚖️ Eligibility
Do you have a valid SIN and legal permission to work in Canada?
We only hire people with legal work status in Canada. A valid work permit, PR, or citizenship is required — no exceptions.
💪 Physical Requirements
Are you comfortable doing active physical work for 6–8 hours a day?
What this involves
Walking constantly between rooms, bending, kneeling, and carrying a backpack or cart weighing around 6 kg throughout the day. You'll be on your feet the entire shift. This is a physically active role.
🚌 Getting Around
Can you travel independently around Toronto to reach client homes?
You'll receive the next day's schedule the evening before. You need to plan your own route and arrive on time. For cleans that are far apart, we arrange a ride between them — that travel time is paid.
🧹 Cleaning Experience
Do you have professional cleaning experience?
Important note
This job requires you to have at least some cleaning experience — in a home, hotel, or cleaning company. You don't need years of it, but we can't hire someone who has never cleaned professionally. Experience in Canada is a strong asset.
We do prefer applicants with some cleaning experience. That said, if you're exceptionally reliable, detail-oriented, and coachable — tell us more about yourself in the next steps. We'll evaluate you on the full picture.
📅 Availability
Which days are you available to work?
What we're looking for
We need at least 5 days per week of availability. We work Monday through Sunday. Candidates who are available on Saturdays are strongly preferred — our Saturday clients are a key part of our schedule.
